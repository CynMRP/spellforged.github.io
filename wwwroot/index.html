About


PROJECT Spellforged is an upcoming, unique Minecraft RPG Experience. Featuring difficult encounters, satisfying combat, and an immersive world to adventure within.

We are determined to host and deliver an incredible Minecraft RPG that all types of players can adventure in. Whether you love to feel the thrill of combat, or explore the world with your friends to find loot and adventure, PROJECT Spellforged will have something in store for everyone to enjoy.



Classes & Abilities

Choose between 5 Classes, each with their own roles within a group, and dozens of abilities to choose from. Mix and match your abilities to create your playstyle, and experience a Class System that is unlimited, yet in-depth.
Play as a Warrior and use powerful weapon attacks to strike down your foes, or study the magicks of Casters to devestate your enemies with either bursts of mana or forbidden dark magics.


Intense Combat

PROJECT Spellforged boasts over 100 Abilities and Passives, each with their own use and upgrade path. Chain together quick Sword attacks to defeat your enemies in a flashy style, or harness the mana around you to launch magical attacks. With unique combat features such as Sprint Dashing, Dual Wielding, and Shields, all players will be able to find their preferred playstyle.
Be careful however. Just how you can wield powerful skills, your foes can have even more devestating attacks...


Powerful Items

While exploring the world and doing Quests, you will come across equipment that will enhance your power, or items that will enhance your equipment.
Use these to your advantage. Refine, Reforge, and Enchant your items to boost your power and give yourself unique strengths found only in this world. If you empower your equipment, you will go from strength to strength continuously.
But be warned, the greatest treasures lie in the darkest depths...
