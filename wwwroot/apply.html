Applying for the Project Team

PROJECT Spellforged is, without a doubt, a grand ambition. Many of us share a positive vision on this project, and want to see it succeed. Help us realize our vision by applying for our Team and joining the project!

Alternatively, you can join our Discord and head to the #team-apply section to apply there.

Some basic requirements to join the team:
  • A Minecraft: Java Edition account. Our server is hosted on Minecraft: Java Edition, so you will not be able to work on the project without an account on Java.
  • A Discord Account. Our team is managed and communicated primarily through Discord. If you need to create an account, head over to Discord to create one.
