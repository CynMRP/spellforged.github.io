Overview


PROJECT Spellforged is an upcoming open-world MMORPG Server for Minecraft: Java Edition. Hailing from mysterious origins and having no recollections of your past, journey through a vast world that's teeming with adventure, and filled with many stories that have yet to be told.

A Vast, Immersive World



The setting of your long journey takes place in the world of Sira. Explore the many regions and nations across the world, take part in open-world activities to claim various treasures, and experience the numerous stories that will slowly unravel the truths of this world and your origins.

Diverse Class Archetypes



Choose from 5 classes, 15 specializations, and over 100 skills to learn and use against your enemies. Each one claims a unique playstyle that aims to fulfill a specific class fantasy, such as rune-drawing Sorcerers and hyper fast Twilights.

Warrior

Frontline solders who focus on using devestating attacks, or taking the brunt of an assault for their allies.

Ranger

Ranged fighters who provide fire support from afar, but occasionally diving in close to deliver swift melee combos.

Caster

Magic conjurers that harness their power into a weapon focus of their choosing, then unleashing it upon their foes either up close or from afar.

Rogue

Agile combatants who aim to eliminate their enemies as swiftly as possible by using a variety of dexterous techniques.

Mystic

A subset of magic users who draw a part of their power from other sources, and combine it with their own to create a unique blend of magic that can support, defend, or attack.
