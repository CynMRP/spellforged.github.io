About

PROJECT Spellforged is an upcoming open-world MMORPG Server for Minecraft: Java Edition. Hailing from unknown origins and no recollections of your past, explore the vast world of Sira, a land teeming with magic and adventures abound. Here, you will claim bountiful treasures, face off against overwhelming foes, and uncover the truths about this world and your origins.

We are determined to host and deliver an incredible Minecraft RPG that all types of players can adventure in. Whether you love to feel the thrill of combat, or exploring the world with your friends to find loot and adventure, PROJECT Spellforged will have something in store for everyone to enjoy.

In-depth Classes

Your journeys will not be without growth. Choose from 5 Classes - each with 3 Specializations - and over 100 Skills to use against your enemies. Each Class and Specialization has a unique playstyle in combat.
So, whether you enjoy being on the frontlines to either decimate your foes or protect your allies, or instead want to provide ranged support to your allies from the back, there is a playstyle that you're bound to find that will keep you engaged and satisfied throughout your adventures.

In-depth Classes

PROJECT Spellforged offers 5 base classes. Each one caters towards baseline roles and playstyles, such as Warriors focused on being frontliners and Casters being adept magic wielders.

Classes are further divided into 3 Specializations, which are the centerpieces of all classes. Each one provides its own unique set of skills, roles, and playstyles. As you explore the world and grow in strength, you'll slowly but surely increase in power as you unlock new skills and upgrades to customize your playstyle even further.

Dynamic Combat

PROJECT Spellforged boasts over 150 skills. Each one has been carefully handmade to not only appear visually striking, but to be powerful and fun to use, as well as being upgradable as you grow your character.
These skills are accompanied by a unique casting system, and intricate combat features such as dashing, shielding, and parrying. All players will be able to find their preferred playstyle with the options presented.
However, your enemies can wield just as devestating attacks, from simple dash strikes to powerful explosions. These, you cannot take alone. Team up with other players to defeat these foes, and be blessed with lost treasures earned through your party's teamwork.


Intense Combat

Combat is a primary focus in PROJECT Spellforged. Our Combat System is diverse and complex, ranging from simple attacks such as firing magic bolts, to dual-wielding Swords and comboing together Abilities to eliminate your foes.

8-skill Casting - Excellent Flow & Efficiency

Casting Skills: [Swap Key] + [Key Bind]

With a heavy focus on combat in PROJECT Spellforged, casting skills needs to be fluid, and needs a good amount of slots for you to assign them to.

PROJECT Spellforged allows you to bind usable skills in 8 skill slots, and cast them in rapid succession using a well-paced casting system. This system allows you to use your Swap Key (Usually keybound to F) to prime an skill cast, then press a respective keybind to activate the skill.

With this system casting all of your skills is 2 key inputs away. The available inputs you have are as followed:

Skill Slot # Key Combination Skill Slot # Key Combination
1 Swap Key + Left Click 5 Sneak + Left Click
2 Swap Key + Right Click 6 Sneak + Right Click
3 Swap Key + Drop Key 7 Sneak + Drop Key
4 Swap Key + Swap Key 8 Sneak + Sneak
Awakened Techniques - Your Ultimate Move

Using an Awakened Technique: Drop + Right Click

At base, Specializations are meant to be simplistic yet fun. However, their core gameplay, playstyle, and enjoyment revolves around their more complex mechanic called an Awakened Technique.

Awakened Techniques grant you a powerful attack or combo to unleash against your enemies that feels extremely fun and satisfying to wield. Each technique has been designed uniquely for each specialization, alongside their own planned upgrades to enhance its power further and to synchronize between your base skills.

So, build up a barrage of windchaser shots and climax with a powerful beam as a Marksman, or call down a legendary blade of light to wield as your own as a Paladin for a short time. Whatever you choose, its strength will not go unnoticed.

Secondary Combat Actions

Combat Actions: Parrying, Shielding, Dashing, Healing

In addition to the numerous skills at your disposal, you will also have access to secondary combat actions depending on your specialization and role.

Combat Action Role Description
Parrying DPS Parrying allows you to block an incoming attack to significantly reduce its damage, and then following up immediately with a counterattack.
Shielding Tanks Shielding allows you to continuously block hits in exchange for being unable to perform most other actions. However, as a tank, it is extremely helpful to allow you to stand your ground.
Healing Supports Healing is a short cooldown action available to all support roles, allowing you to heal a single ally or a group of allies around you. This is in addition to other, more powerful support and healing skills.
Dashing All Dashing grants you a short burst of speed in order to quickly dodge an incoming attack, which can be the main determinant in a life or death situation.

Expansive Item System

Items and their strengths are important in PROJECT Spellforged. Many items you will find in the world have a use or purpose besides being sold to a vendor for money. The system as a whole is also designed to be simplistic and easy to pick up, but also to have a fair expanse over what you can use and combine across different types of equipment you may find.

In addition to the standard weapons and armor that you will find on your travels, there are some other key items and features that you should keep an eye out for.

Offhands - Role-defining Items

Available Offhands: Parry Weapons, Shields, Sigils

Depending on what role you take on in a party, whether a DPS or a Tank, you'll be able to equip an offhand to suit your role.

Offhands will grant you secondary combat actions depending on which one you're using. These actions are incredibly handy to performing your role. However, an offhand for one role cannot be used on other roles.


Accessories - Role-dependent Benefits

Accessory Types: Rings, Bracelets, Necklaces, Earrings

While Weapons grant you damage output and Armor improves your defenses and attributes, Accessories are a third equipment type that will allow you to enhance your character in a different style.

Accessories give you unique attributes not found on other equipment types, such as Resilience, Tenacity, and Devotion. These stats can affect your playstyle depending on what you wear. Furthermore, upgrading your Accessories via Refinements will increase these stats to grant you more power.

When exploring the world, be sure to look out for these kinds of items. Accessories can be helpful to your playstyle depending on what you prefer.

Refinements - Further Empowerment

Enhancement Styles: Reforges, Refinements, Imbuements, Enchantments

Enemies are considerably strong and dangerous in this world. If you feel like your equipment is lackluster and are unable to acquire more, refinements are a quick, yet reliable way to improve its strength.

Refinements upgrade your equipment's base stats directly. They can improve a weapon's damage, an armor piece's defensive stats, or an accessory's main stat. Equipment can be refined multiple times at the cost of either Refinement Materials, or using other Equipment.
