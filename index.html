About

PROJECT Spellforged is an upcoming open-world MMORPG Server for Minecraft: Java Edition. Hailing from unknown origins and no recollections of your past, explore the vast world of Sira, a land teeming with magic and adventures abound. Here, you will claim bountiful treasures, face off against overwhelming foes, and uncover the truths about this world and your origins.

We are determined to host and deliver an incredible Minecraft RPG that all types of players can adventure in. Whether you love to feel the thrill of combat, or exploring the world with your friends to find loot and adventure, PROJECT Spellforged will have something in store for everyone to enjoy.

In-depth Classes

Your journeys will not be without growth. Choose from 5 Classes - each with 3 Specializations - and over 100 Skills to use against your enemies. Each Class and Specialization has a unique playstyle in combat.
So, whether you enjoy being on the frontlines to either decimate your foes or protect your allies, or instead want to provide ranged support to your allies from the back, there is a playstyle that you're bound to find that will keep you engaged and satisfied throughout your adventures.

In-depth Classes

PROJECT Spellforged offers 5 base classes. Each one caters towards baseline roles and playstyles, such as Warriors focused on being frontliners and Casters being adept magic wielders.

Classes are further divided into 3 Specializations, which are the centerpieces of all classes. Each one provides its own unique set of skills, roles, and playstyles. As you explore the world and grow in strength, you'll slowly but surely increase in power as you unlock new skills and upgrades to customize your playstyle even further.

Dynamic Combat

PROJECT Spellforged boasts over 150 skills. Each one has been carefully handmade to not only appear visually striking, but to be powerful and fun to use, as well as being upgradable as you grow your character.
These skills are accompanied by a unique casting system, and intricate combat features such as dashing, shielding, and parrying. All players will be able to find their preferred playstyle with the options presented.
However, your enemies can wield just as devestating attacks, from simple dash strikes to powerful explosions. These, you cannot take alone. Team up with other players to defeat these foes, and be blessed with lost treasures earned through your party's teamwork.


Intense Combat

Combat is a primary focus in PROJECT Spellforged. Our Combat System is diverse and complex, ranging from simple attacks such as firing magic bolts, to dual-wielding Swords and comboing together Abilities to eliminate your foes.

The F-System - Efficient Skills Casting

Casting Skills: [Swap Key] + [Key Bind]

With over 100 Abilities and Passives combined in PROJECT Spellforged, casting those Abilities needs to be fluid, and needs a good amount of Ability Slots for you to assign them to.

PROJECT Spellforged allows you to bind usable skills in 8 skill slots, and cast them in rapid succession using a well-paced casting system. This system allows you to use your Swap Key (Usually keybound to F) to prime an ability cast, then press a respective keybind to activate the skill. Passives do not take up an Ability Slot.

With this system casting all of your skills is 2 key inputs away. The available inputs you have are as followed:

Skill Slot # Key Combination Skill Slot # Key Combination
1 Swap Key + Left Click 5 Sneak + Left Click
2 Swap Key + Right Click 6 Sneak + Right Click
3 Swap Key + Drop Key 7 Sneak + Drop Key
4 Swap Key + Swap Key 8 Sneak + Sneak
Dual-Wielding & Shields - The Offhand Slot Reborn

Equipping an Offhand Item: Place it in your Offhand Slot manually via your Inventory.

Offhanding in Minecraft is an underappreciated mechanic. We intend to make it both useful and important by implementing Dual-Wielding and Shields into PROJECT Spellforged.

Dual-Wielding allows you to attack with 2 weapons in both your Mainhand and your Offhand. You can swing with your Offhand by Right Clicking, and certain Game Options allow you to switch that up if you're not a fan of it. This allows you to experience melee combat in a unique way, and makes certain weapons such as Daggers - weak in damage but swift in speed - especially viable.

Shields are present in PROJECT Spellforged, remade in a way to fit a proper RPG. Use Shields to defend against powerful attacks, or to push back with special Shield Skills that can be used to counter these attacks. Shields are especially good for a Tanking role in your party, so it is advised for you to use one if you intend to hold the line.

Sprint Dashing - Combat Movement Enhanced

Using Dash: Start Sprinting

Movement can be a large reason of why combat is fun and intense. Some enemy attacks may need both speed and reaction time in order to evade them.

PROJECT Spellforged has implemented a simple, yet useful mechanic called Sprint Dashing. This will give you a large, quick-fading burst of speed when you begin sprinting. This allows you to dodge attacks faster and in a stylized manner.

Sprint Dashing isn't unlimited though, and has a short cooldown attached to it. Be sure you time it wisely, otherwise you may find yourself taking a hit you won't be able to withstand...

Expansive Item System

Items and their strengths are important in PROJECT Spellforged. Many items you will find in the world have a use or purpose besides being sold to a vendor for money.

Accessories - The Third Equipment Type

Accessory Types: Trinkets, Gemstones, Rings, Charms

While Weapons grant you damage output and Armor improves your defenses and Attributes, Accessories are a third equipment type that will allow you to enhance your character in a different style.

Accessories give you unique Stats not typically found on other equipment types, such as Mana Regen, Critical Strike Chance, and Threat Generation. These stats can affect your playstyle depending on what you wear. Furthermore, upgrading your Accessories via Refinements will increase these stats to grant you more power.

When exploring the world, be sure to look out for these kinds of items. Accessories can be crucial to your playstyle depending on what you need the most.

Item Enhancements - Customize your Equipment

Enhancement Styles: Reforges, Refinements, Imbuements, Enchantments

You will not only find normal Equipment in the world, but also items and places that can be used to enhance them, such as Refinement Materials or Enchantment Altars. The power that these can give to your equipment can effectively change or enhance how you play, ranging from granting you additional Attributes or special bonuses.

  • Reforges allow you to tune your equipment's stats to your desire. They convert a percentage amount of a stat you select on an item into an Attribute stat of your choice, allowing you to specialize further into an Attribute. This is less of an upgrade and more of a tuning to help turn your equipment into what you want it to be.
  • Refinements upgrade your equipment's base stats directly. They can improve a Weapon's Damage, an Armor piece's defensive stats, or an Accessory's main stat. Equipment can be refined multiple times at the cost of either Refinement Materials, or using other Equipment.
  • Imbuements grant your equipment additional stats by imbuing it with specific materials. Weapons, Armor, and Accessories can be imbued, allowing you to effectively stack imbuements together to become stronger.
  • Enchantments are unique empowerments of your equipment. Rather than improving its stats, Enchantments grant you special abilities while you wear the equipment that has them. You can obtain them by finding Enchantment Altars out in the world, and offering specific items to enchant. Equipment can only have 1 Enchantment on them at a time.

Using Equipment - Nonrestrictive System

What you can use: Everything!

As you fill up your Bank, you will find many types of Equipment and Weapons. PROJECT Spellforged has very few restrictions on equipment usage, allowing you to create diverse item builds.

All classes can melee attack with all Weapon Types and equip all types of armor. Utilize Bows as a Warrior to pick off enemies from afar, or save yourself a Dagger as a Caster for your last resort.

There are only 2 kinds of restrictions to wearing equipment: Level Requirement and Equipment Weight.

  • All Equipment has a Level Requirement attached to them. Not meeting this requirement will prevent you from using the equipment, and you will not gain any stats from them.
  • Equipment Weight is a flexible system where Weapons and Armor have a Weight value attached to them, and each class has a preset Weapon and Armor Weight maximum. These values add on whenever you are using them, and going over the capacity of either your Weapon Weight or Armor Weight will invoke penalties. However, they don't prevent you from dual-wielding heavy weapons...
