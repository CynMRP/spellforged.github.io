The world is filled with many small details to help immerse you into yours surroundings. In addition, its environments are enhanced by an original soundtrack that you can listen to in-game.

As you explore, you will be accompanied by ambient tunes that inspire wonder and serenity. As you fight, you will be aided by intense, face-paced tracks to keep you motivated. No matter where your journeys take you, know that you will always have the right music to elevate your moments.

Key Features

Listen to a variety of musical styles, tailored to each setting and situation.

Professional Compositions

Each soundtrack is composed using professional tools and libraries, no noteblocks included. Once completed, it's then recorded and placed in-game for you to enjoy.

Regional Playlists

While exploring the world or engaging in combat, the soundtrack played is selected from a dedicated playlist for each area, allowing you to listen to different music in every venture or encounter.

Thematic Variety

All soundtracks are made with different themes in mind, including relaxing melodies for traveling, all-out movements for intense showdowns, and emotional pieces for narrative moments.
