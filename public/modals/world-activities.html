Open-world Activities

Activity Types

Combat Puzzles Minigames

If you need encouragement to explore, there are many open-world activities that you can take part in. Clear out enemy locales, solve puzzles, and take part in minigames that will all give you rewards for completion.

These activities can be done alone or with other players, and you will all be rewarded the same! Explore the world and see what activities you can find to complete together.

Activity Examples

Find various activities to complete and reap the rewards from.

Combat Encounters

Instanced combat encounters ranging from waves of enemies to boss fights. Complete them either alone or with friends to gain valuable loot, or trigger other events nearby.

Runic Sequences

A sequence of floating runes etched along walls in various ruin sites. Re-order them correctly to unlock nearby caches or activities.

Treasure Scopes

Special telescopes that can spot faint glints on the ground. Use one to search your surroundings and find any treasure that's buried into the ground.
