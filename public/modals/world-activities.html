Open-World Activities

Treasures Abound

Activity Types:

Combat Puzzles Minigames

Should you need encouragement to explore, there are various open-world activities that you can take part in for a fun experience and lucrative rewards. Clear out enemy camps and take their treasures for yourself, solve puzzles ranging from simple to complex so you can reap the rewards from the solution, or just take part in fun minigames that will give you loot just as easily.

These activities can either be done alone or with a group of friends, and all of you will be rewarded just the same! Venture the world and see what kinds of activities you can find to complete together.

Activity Rewards

Complete various activities to earn valuable rewards for your adventure.

Combat Encounters

Defeat enemy camps and dangerous foes to claim their treasures as your own.

Rewards:
  • Rare equipment
  • Combat experience
  • Territory control
Puzzles & Riddles

Use your wit to solve challenging puzzles hidden throughout the landscape.

Rewards:
  • Ancient artifacts
  • Hidden knowledge
  • Special abilities
Fun Minigames

Take a break from your adventure with entertaining diversions and still earn rewards.

Rewards:
  • Unique collectibles
  • Currency bonuses
  • Consumable items
