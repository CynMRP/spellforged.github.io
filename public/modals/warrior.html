Primary Weapons: Swords, Axes, Spears

Warriors strive for excellence in armed combat, and fighting on the frontlines for their allies. Their natural resilience allows them to stand headfirst against their enemies, and their strength enables them to use the heaviest equipment available to them.

Specialization Details

Slayer

Slayers use their great strength to unleash powerful, blazing attacks, wiping out their enemies with a destructive force.

Key Abilities:
  • Blazing Strike
  • Destructive Force
  • Power Surge
Stats Focus:
  • Strength
  • Critical Rate
  • Health
Berserker

Berserkers are ferocious fighters, utilizing their rage to cleave down their opponents and weaken their attacks.

Key Abilities:
  • Rage Mode
  • Crushing Blow
  • Battle Fury
Stats Focus:
  • Attack Power
  • Rage Generation
  • Defense
Lancer

Lancers slash and thrust at their opponents to inflict fatal wounds with a devastating, frigid power.

Key Abilities:
  • Piercing Thrust
  • Frost Lance
  • Shield Wall
Stats Focus:
  • Pierce Power
  • Ice Damage
  • Block Rate
