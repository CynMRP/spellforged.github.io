Refinements

Enhancement Styles:

Reforges Refinements Imbuements Enchantments

Enemies are considerably strong and dangerous in this world. If you feel like your equipment is lackluster and are unable to acquire more, refinements are a quick, yet reliable way to improve its strength.

Refinements upgrade your equipment's base stats directly. They can improve a weapon's damage, an armor piece's defensive stats, or an accessory's main stat. Equipment can be refined multiple times at the cost of either Refinement Materials, or using other Equipment.

Enhancement Methods

Reforges

Completely rebuilds an item with better materials to improve its base quality.

Benefits:
  • Increases item rarity tier
  • Improves stat ranges
  • Can unlock additional attribute slots
Rare Materials 50% Success Rate
Refinements

Strengthens an item's existing properties through direct enhancement of base stats.

Benefits:
  • Increases weapon damage
  • Improves armor defense
  • Enhances accessory primary attributes
Common Materials 90% Success Rate
Imbuements

Infuses items with elemental or magical properties to add special effects.

Benefits:
  • Adds elemental damage
  • Creates special on-hit effects
  • Grants resistance to specific damage types
Elemental Materials 75% Success Rate
Enchantments

Applies magical modifications that add unique properties beyond basic attributes.

Benefits:
  • Adds proc effects
  • Grants utility bonuses
  • Provides conditional stat boosts
Magical Essences 65% Success Rate

Refinement Process

Material Refinement

Use specialized refinement materials gathered from the world to enhance your equipment.

Material Sources:
  • Dungeon rewards
  • Elite enemy drops
  • Resource gathering
Safe Method Low Cost
Equipment Sacrifice

Sacrifice unwanted equipment to transfer their power to your preferred items.

Effectiveness Factors:
  • Sacrificed item quality
  • Item type compatibility
  • Refinement level of sacrificed item
Higher Potential Medium Risk
Refinement Levels

Each refinement level provides increasing benefits but requires more resources.

Level Progression:
  • +1 to +5: Common refinements
  • +6 to +10: Advanced refinements
  • +11 to +15: Masterwork refinements
Success Rate Decreases Costs Increase
