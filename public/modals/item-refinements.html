Refinements

Enemies are considerably strong and dangerous in this world. If you feel like your equipment is lackluster and are unable to acquire more, refinements are a quick, yet reliable way to improve its strength.

Refinements upgrade your equipment's base stats directly. They can improve a weapon's damage, an armor piece's defensive stats, or an accessory's main stat. Equipment can be refined multiple times at the cost of either mefinement materials, or scrapping other equipment.

Process Details

Learn about the process of refining items to consider how it will affect your gameplay and progression.

Material Refinement

Find refinement materials out in the world from chests, enemies and instanced content, and use them to refine and level your equipment.

Equipment Recycling

Once an equipment piece has outlived its use, scrap it to gain refinements materials to use on newer items.

Refinement Levels

Every item can be refined to increase its power, but its maximum level depends on its rarity. The higher the rarity, the more it can be refined.
