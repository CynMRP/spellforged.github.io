Refinements

Enemies are considerably strong and dangerous in this world. If you feel like your equipment is lackluster and are unable to acquire more, refinements are a quick, yet reliable way to improve its strength.

Refinements upgrade your equipment's base stats directly. They can improve a weapon's damage, an armor piece's defensive stats, or an accessory's main stat. Equipment can be refined multiple times at the cost of either Refinement Materials, or using other Equipment.

Refinement Process

Material Refinement

Use specialized refinement materials gathered from the world to enhance your equipment.

Material Sources:
  • Dungeon rewards
  • Elite enemy drops
  • Resource gathering
Safe Method Low Cost
Equipment Sacrifice

Sacrifice unwanted equipment to transfer their power to your preferred items.

Effectiveness Factors:
  • Sacrificed item quality
  • Item type compatibility
  • Refinement level of sacrificed item
Higher Potential Medium Risk
Refinement Levels

Each refinement level provides increasing benefits but requires more resources.

Level Progression:
  • +1 to +5: Common refinements
  • +6 to +10: Advanced refinements
  • +11 to +15: Masterwork refinements
Success Rate Decreases Costs Increase
