Mystic



Primary Weapons: Catalysts, Bows, Swords

Mystics are the primary support of the group, revitalizing or empowering their allies using various holy magicks. In some cases, they can fight on the frontlines, using their divine magic to strike down their foes, or their innate protections to aid them in defending against dangerous enemies.
