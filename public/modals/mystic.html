Mystics are the primary support of the group, revitalizing or empowering their allies using various holy magicks. In some cases, they can fight on the frontlines, using their divine magic to strike down their foes, or their innate protections to aid them in defending against dangerous enemies.

Spiritualist - Weapon: Catalyst Roles: DPS | Support

Spiritualists fight in tandem with their spirit companion, creating a fluid bond that allows them to cast either powerful offensive or supportive spells.

Flow

Cast a water bolt straight towards the targeted location, dealing Magical Water Damage.

After using Flow and Mirror: Flow, Interflow becomes available.

Synced Cooldown 6.5 seconds
Range 16 blocks
Radius 4 blocks (Circle)
I'll Help Too!

Passively, your spirit will target the enemy you hit and throw water bolts at them every 0.4 seconds, dealing Magical Water Damage, consuming 1 Raindrop and granting 1 Bubble.
Your spirit will un-target this enemy once it's defeated or is out of range.

Use this skill while looking at an enemy to set them as your spirit's current target.

Cooldown 1 second
Range 16 blocks
Radius 3 blocks (Circle)
Unification

Begin to unify your magic with your spirit for 10 seconds.

While active, Flow, Mirror: Flow and Interflow are transformed into Unify: Self, Unify: Spirit and As One: Convergence respectively.
Additionally, all other skills are disabled, and Flow and Mirror: Flow have their cooldowns increased by 3 seconds.
