Secondary Combat Actions



Combat Actions: Parrying, Shielding, Dashing, Healing

In addition to the numerous skills at your disposal, you will also have access to secondary combat actions depending on your specialization and role.

Combat Action Types

Combat Action Role Description
Parrying
DPS Parrying allows you to block an incoming attack to significantly reduce its damage, and then following up immediately with a counterattack.
Shielding
Tanks Shielding allows you to continuously block hits in exchange for being unable to perform most other actions. However, as a tank, it is extremely helpful to allow you to stand your ground.
Healing
Supports Healing is a short cooldown action available to all support roles, allowing you to heal a single ally or a group of allies around you. This is in addition to other, more powerful support and healing skills.
Dashing
All Dashing grants you a short burst of speed in order to quickly dodge an incoming attack, which can be the main determinant in a life or death situation.
