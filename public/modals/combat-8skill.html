Casting Mechanics



With how deeply designed each specialization is, it demands casting mechanics that allows all of its skills to be used without any tradeoffs.

These mechanics include 8 2-input key combinations, and skills that transform into auxillary skills when certain conditions are met. Left Click and Right Click can also transform into these skills.

As a result, specializations can have up to 10 skill keybinds, but their total skill count is able to number more than that. This results in them being able to have a large number of skills that are always available for use, which in turn allows for further unique gameplay and complexity.

Auxillary Skills

Unassignable skills that become available when other skills transform into them after meeting certain conditions.

Kindled Flourish
Slayer Skill

Slash upwards around you, dealing Physical Fire Damage.

※ Right Click transforms into Kindled Flourish after using all Searing skills or Blazing skills.
※ Kindled Flourish is followed up by Burning Flourish.

Radius 5 blocks (Circle)
Write: Fire
Sorcerer Skill

Write a Fire rune, progressing Write: Origin.

※ Flamebolt transforms into Write: Fire while in a rune drawing state.
※ Write: Fire can only be used if the next rune to write is Fire.

Swirling Cannonball
Spiritualist Skill

Lob a large ball of swirling water at the target location that bursts violently, dealing Magical Water Damage and additional Magical Water Damage per water droplet absorbed using Absorb.

Becomes unavailable on use.

Grants 1 Soul Bond.

※ Send: Cannonball transforms into Swirling Cannonball after absorption mode ends.

Range 16 blocks
Radius 6 blocks

NOTICE: Skills shown are subject to change.
