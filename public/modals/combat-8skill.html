Versatile Casting



With how deeply designed each specialization is, it demands a casting system that allows its many skills to be used without any tradeoffs.

To accomplish this, our casting system makes use of 8 skill keybinds, and skills that transform into auxillary skills when certain requirements are met. Some skills will even transform your Left Click and Right Click buttons into auxillary skills temporarily.

You can expect to have 9 to 10 skill keybinds to use at a time, but your total skill count can number more than that. This results in a unique setup that allows for a specialization to have a large number of skills available to them consistently, which in turn allows for further unique gameplay and complexity.
