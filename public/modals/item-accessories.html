Accessories

While Weapons grant you damage output and Armor improves your defenses and attributes, Accessories are a third equipment type that will allow you to enhance your character in a different style.

Accessories give you unique attributes not found on other equipment types, such as Resilience, Tenacity, and Devotion. These stats can affect your playstyle depending on what you wear. Furthermore, upgrading your Accessories via Refinements will increase these stats to grant you more power.

When exploring the world, be sure to look out for these kinds of items. Accessories can be helpful to your playstyle depending on what you prefer.

Special Attributes

Accessories provide unique attributes that aren't found on other equipment types.

Resilience

Increases your ability to withstand continuous damage and reduces the duration of negative effects.

Benefits:
  • Reduces debuff duration
  • Improves sustained survivability
  • Enhances recovery from crowd control
Tenacity

Improves your resistance to critical strikes and enhances your ability to perform under pressure.

Benefits:
  • Reduces critical damage taken
  • Improves performance at low health
  • Enhances defensive capabilities
Devotion

Strengthens your connection to magical energies and enhances support capabilities.

Benefits:
  • Improves healing output
  • Enhances buff duration
  • Increases resource regeneration rates

NOTICE: All features are subject to change.
