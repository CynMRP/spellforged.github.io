World Structure

The world that you will be exploring has been cultivated in a way to encourage you to explore and learn more about it. The scale of each region is massive, and scattered across them are are numerous points of interest and content.

To help you traverse it all, certain features exist that make doing so seem much more feasible. And as you progress through the world, you'll slowly gain access to more means of making your travels easier.

Key Features

Some of the key features about the world's design and how to progress through it.

Open-world Environment

The world made is a complete, open-world environment with few limits on where you can go. Each area is populated with numerous locations, quests and content to encourage you to explore it all.

Story-driven Progression

The main progression comes in the form of story-driven mainline quests that will grant you access to later content and regions, all while unfolding the greater story of the world. Smaller anecdotes are told through side quests and lore passages.

Pleasant Travels

Traveling comes with a handful of quality-of-life features that make it much more pleasant to do, including transportation, increased movement speed and swim speed, higher block step height, and more!
