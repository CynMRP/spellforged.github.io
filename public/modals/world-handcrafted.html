World Structure

The world that you will be exploring is not only vast and beautiful, but also filled with history and secrets to uncover. You will be stepping into it with no recollection of who you are or where you came from, so why not try and not only learn about this world, but also who you truly are?

World Features

Explore a richly detailed environment with countless stories to discover.

Open-world Environment

The world made is vast, and open for exploration with very few limits on where you can go. Each region is populated with numerous enemies, quests and bite-sized content to encourage you to explore it all.

Story-driven Progression

The main progression comes in the form of story-driven mainline quests, in which you will learn about the greater story of this world. Smaller anecdotes are told through side quests and hidden lore found throughout the world.

Quality-of-life Travel Features

Traveling comes with a handful of features that make it much more pleasant to do, including transportation, increased movement speed and swim speed, higher block step height, and more!
