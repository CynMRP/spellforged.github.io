A Handcrafted World

Historical yet Undiscovered

Design Philosophy:

Open-world

The world that you will be exploring is not only vast and beautiful, but also filled with history and secrets to uncover. You will be stepping into it with no recollection of who you are or where you came from, so why not try and not only learn about this world, but also who you truly are?

Experience the main tale of this world and your involvement through story-driven mainline quests and meeting unique characters along the way. Learn more about its intricacies through side quests, lore entries known as Collectibles, or just by observing and talking to the inhabitants of towns and villages.

World Features

Explore a richly detailed environment with countless stories to discover.

Rich Lore

Uncover the deep history of the realm through ancient artifacts, scrolls, and local legends.

Discover:
  • Ancient civilizations
  • Forgotten heroes
  • Mythical creatures
Diverse Landscapes

Traverse through varied terrains from lush forests to desolate wastelands, each with unique challenges.

Explore:

Memorable Characters

Forge relationships with unique inhabitants, each with their own motivations and stories.

Interact with:
  • Complex companions
