Rogue



Specializations

Assassin Twilight Reaper

Rogues excel in close quarter combat, boasting high versatility and movement. Their preferred weapons give them the ability to perform swift attacks constantly. In combat, Rogues have the option to stealth in to assassinate their targets, or go all out and slash with speed and precision at their enemies.

Twilight - Weapon: Sword Roles: DPS

Twilights move with haste and strike with lightning, unleashing torrents of slashes to quickly dice up their enemies.

Hyperslash

Rapidly slash your sword in front of you 5 times, dealing Physical Lightning Damage.

When used, Hyperslash's cooldown is increased by 0.5 seconds multiplied by the next Blade Stage you will be on.

※ Hyperslash is followed up by Hyperthrust.

Cooldown 3 seconds
Radius 5 blocks (Cone)
Blade Draw

Draw your blade and quickly slash 7 times in front of you, causing echoes of them to slice in front of you dealing Physical Lightning Damage.

Becomes unavailable upon use.

Bladework Cost 1
Range 4 blocks
Radius 5 blocks (Circle)
Storm's Wake

Prepare your blade and then unleash 2 powerful slashes in front of you that echo forward, dealing Physical Lightning Damage.

※ Storm's Wake can only be used after using 5 Blade Skills within one Blade Stage.

Channel Time 0.75 seconds
Radius 7 blocks (Cone)

Reaper - Weapon: Scythe Roles: DPS

Reapers revel in the shadows and in death, using both to siphon and ravage their victims.
