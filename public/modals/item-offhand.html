Offhands

Available Offhands:

Parry Weapons Shields Sigils

Depending on what role you take on in a party, whether a DPS or a Tank, you'll be able to equip an offhand to suit your role.

Offhands will grant you secondary combat actions depending on which one you're using. These actions are incredibly handy to performing your role. However, an offhand for one role cannot be used on other roles.

Tank Protection

Offhand Types

Parry Weapons

Secondary weapons designed for DPS roles to deflect attacks and counter with quick strikes.

Role Benefits:
  • DPS Combat Action: Parrying
  • Increased Critical Strike
  • Counter-attack Damage
DPS Only
Shields

Defensive equipment for Tank roles that absorb damage and protect allies.

Role Benefits:
  • Tank Combat Action: Shielding
  • Damage Reduction
  • Area Protection
Tank Only
Sigils

Magical artifacts used by Support roles to enhance healing and provide buffs.

Role Benefits:
  • Support Combat Action: Healing
  • Enhanced Mana Regeneration
  • Area of Effect Buffs
Support Only
