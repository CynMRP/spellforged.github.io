Applying for the Project Team

PROJECT Spellforged is, without a doubt, a grand ambition. Many of us share a positive vision on this project, and want to see it succeed.

We are looking for people who wish to share in this vision, and wish to join us in bringing it to life. You can do so by applying for the project and joining our team! You will join into an organized and passionate group of people who are working together to bring this project towards excellency. We also value friendly ties and having fun, so expect meet new people and make new acquaintances.

Alternatively, you can join our Discord and head to the #team-applications section to apply there.

Some basic requirements to join the team:
  • A Minecraft: Java Edition account. Our server is hosted on Minecraft: Java Edition, so you will not be able to work on the project without an account on Java.
  • A Discord Account. Our team is managed and communicated primarily through Discord. If you need to create an account, head over to Discord to create one.
  • A strong understanding of the English language. PROJECT Spellforged and its team communicate primarily through English.
